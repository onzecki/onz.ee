My taste in music!

My music taste is quite varied - however, by far my favorite music genre is Breakcore!

Breakcore is quite a difficult genre to define but I mainly prefer older, rougher tracks think -
Venetian Snares or Shitmat.

Modern Breakcore is also up there, however I still largely prefer more aggressive tracks think -
bye2 or Machine Girl.

Other similar artists I like include : Squarepusher, Nero's Day at Disneyland, Metaroom, Rory in early 20s, Vertigoaway, Emray, femtanyl and Romdom!

I also like more experimental music overall think -
Aphex Twin.

I'm a fan of speedcore and similar songs, think -
Diabarha or Kobaryo.

However I also like much chiller songs, think -
Windows 96, Fakear or Emancipator.

As of late, I've mainly been listening to: Venetian Snares!

He has been one of my favorite artists for a while, as of late however, I've had My Half on loop!

It's a song that manages to perfectly describe my definition of breakcore, and is just incredibly well made.
