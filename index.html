My taste in music!

My music taste is quite varied - however, by far my favorite music genre is Breakcore!

Breakcore is quite a difficult genre to define but I mainly prefer older, rougher tracks think -
Venetian Snares or Shitmat.

Modern Breakcore is also up there, however I still largely prefer more aggressive tracks think -
bye2 or Machine Girl.

Other similar artists I like include : Squarepusher, Nero's Day at Disneyland, Metaroom, Rory in early 20s, Vertigoaway, Emray and femtanyl

I also like more experimental music overall think -
Aphex Twin.

I'm a fan of very aggressive songs, think -
Diabarha or Kobaryo.

However I also like much chiller songs, think -
Windows 96, Fakear or Emancipator.

As of late, I've been mainly listening to: Romdom!

They're a very underrated artist, please check them out.

My favorite song by Romdom is Deliver Us (The Culprit), which, as of writing, has a singular like (made by me), please check them out!
